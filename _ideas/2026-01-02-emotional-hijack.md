Emotional hijack:  
When emotions & beliefs about how things should be override reason.  
  
Recognize it.  
Pause.  
Say “I’m not feeling well” & step away.  
No replies or messages for 48h.  
Do not communicate while you are in this state!  
  
Thank me later!
