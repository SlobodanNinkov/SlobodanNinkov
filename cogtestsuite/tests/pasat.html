PASAT

Paced Auditory Serial Addition Test

A demanding working-memory task using paced arithmetic.

  • Single-digit numbers appear one at a time on screen.
  • For each new number, enter the sum of that number and the previous number.
  • Press Enter or click Submit after each answer.
  • You must answer before the next number appears.
Example:
3 appears → (no answer yet)
7 appears → enter 10 (3+7)
2 appears → enter 9 (7+2)
