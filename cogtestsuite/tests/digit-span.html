Digit Span Test

This test measures your verbal working-memory span.

  • Digits will appear one at a time on screen.
  • Forward: Type them back in the same order.
  • Backward: Type them back in reverse order.
  • Sequences start at 3 digits and grow longer.
  • Two trials per length; the test ends after both trials at a length fail.
