Sternberg Item-Recognition

Sternberg Task

Tests how quickly and accurately you can match items in memory.

  • A short list of digits (memory set) will appear.
  • Memorise them — they disappear after 2 seconds.
  • Then probe digits appear one at a time.
  • Click YES if the probe was in the list, NO if not.
  • Respond as quickly and accurately as possible.
  • Three set sizes (2, 4, 6) are tested.
