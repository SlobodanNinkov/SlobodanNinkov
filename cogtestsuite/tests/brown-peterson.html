Brown–Peterson Task

Demonstrates rapid forgetting of unrehearsed information.

  • Three consonants will appear briefly — memorise them.
  • You will then answer simple arithmetic problems as a distractor.
  • After a set delay (3 – 18 s) you will be asked to recall the letters.
  • Six trials at increasing delays show the forgetting curve.
